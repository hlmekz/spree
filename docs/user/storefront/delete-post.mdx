---
title: "Delete a Blog Post"
description: "Delete a blog post and manage your entire blog using Spree Commerce."
---

At some point, you might want to delete a post if it’s outdated, no longer relevant, or doesn’t align with your current content strategy.

To delete a post, navigate to the Posts tab of the admin dashboard found under Storefront.

<img height="359" width="624" src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXe0AsJO3UWEcw6S0X1QbMqUxQji0NNchNkGSfRyRt_6Xqp4wMUQIWwvxsSU4bswpBq81NrtJ3zhmVwmv9W0gD0E9d3hJ0dmV-BqyM05hjkmjKRU5i4_G8kb_4EUaaF0HPQSTax1HQ?key=xlFyZS8Qk0f4G156jvjemdwP" />

Find the post you’d like to delete and click on it to open the post editor for that specific post.

<img height="359" width="624" src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfErSOqnsWdDYAh96m8VDxBvbaqCyWi_oeGXPHWC56GQCKLHK7QljYdlDUYOHWUcbtiDEmLDS1RLF-lmzeU164N7Zp3Po1kQBYcQVC-N0mbdlfR7sa7M7JX-s-Cs1f77e54K5Q-bQ?key=xlFyZS8Qk0f4G156jvjemdwP" />

Scroll to the bottom of the page and locate the red ‘Delete’ button in the bottom right corner under SEO settings.

<img height="341" width="624" src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXc-o8V9kia6VmHiLz63zddORBJ4jEmMFIf2pOX8nDfJEwI1STfR7UROsPr3FHClNiLGMd6eNCznOTRO5pjxCo898vUAnFRC_OYiFsPUJzMid5ILCxC4caN9tc4ayQ1USWh4YchWCg?key=xlFyZS8Qk0f4G156jvjemdwP" />

Click ‘Delete’ and the post will be removed from your site.
