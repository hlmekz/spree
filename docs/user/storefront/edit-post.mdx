title: "Edit a Blog Post"
description: "Edit your blog post and manage your blog using Spree Commerce"
---

At some point, you might want to edit a blog post after it’s published to ensure the information remains accurate and up-to-date. Additionally, updating the content can help improve SEO performance and keep it relevant for your audience.

To edit a post, navigate to the Posts tab of the admin dashboard found under Storefront.

<img height="359" width="624" src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXdzpkcEBvDfI-yRJotgQybdpnJGSm_r-k4fADhfJ73tIeTgYJjlzRUisRbWF7gayvY3JqPka6FkGbOCIxEV2nHhvJoMX8ya9RBj3dzj8GAauDqFekJ_c1cPIsZCHuK2UbqySDlZ4g?key=4-c_ViISBPAJksS8C7WalMUx" />

Find the post you’d like to edit and click on it. This will open the post editor for that specific post.

<img height="359" width="624" src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXe4WHPfm5wod3sN8MJuRINTxbBDdbNPsvEG2TtV34a-Rbzlvvrb1xg2gTyaPEJtrfeUrdu0Lf326u2_1OeSdGh3W0EHV5epYdqUw54-Uz8qjBFixAZEEsIg1axlpccBZq1wUXbGag?key=4-c_ViISBPAJksS8C7WalMUx" />

Feel free to edit any of the content or settings visible on the page.

Once you’re done, make sure to click the blue ‘Update’ button at the bottom of the page, otherwise any changes you’ve made will not be saved.

<img height="383" width="624" src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfpltKT1FLx3Ru8q2YlZfDizDQeU7STtRdA4UdSRpNkKrWE5ZlTJHL7wGOywC6I9lv5Ue4-wc6n8Fyr8_6f42i054p4XvjhlZoxDmwuNmTAIbTQdIrPzK63eUHqsrZvJ0Z1VGtQiQ?key=4-c_ViISBPAJksS8C7WalMUx" />

Congratulations, you’ve just edited a post!
