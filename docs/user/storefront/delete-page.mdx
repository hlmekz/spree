---
title: "Delete a Landing Page"
description: "Delete a landing page you no longer need."
---

When a page is no longer needed, you can remove it to keep your backend organized and clutter-free.

To delete a page, navigate to the Pages tab of the admin dashboard, found under Storefront.

<img height="359" width="624" src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXd8ijBrIUNPcDGN6vDiQY4FFuLBazMRGm2Rc-qKjmZVfF9r9kt0pitdjE5f_WjqFpzG5I4XPo5m-8DRfbUvc0I2s1gfBGE4n9WcqBEIXsCYr5GIxh3oOmCB9wdGVXVuW40moeLM?key=wFDXEcsw7QqwrKfLfsp4Zxsi" />

Find the page you want to delete and click on it. This will open the Theme Editor for that specific page.

<img height="359" width="624" src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXda6b7WQ0TyAfVqbvBRQ8yT51cWYrZaDVfh6pf6q8VKnF8Id_s7Xsl7_Q-r3Vvj5MkbeVcCwzOW7-fjv4Nu3oqirVSQE7MBcItaTYlio-ZSUzkMBIjz4rgUWM0wWGfvbNKA2EPJfw?key=wFDXEcsw7QqwrKfLfsp4Zxsi" />

Click on ‘Page Settings’ found on the right hand side of the top bar Theme Editor. This will open the page settings window.

<img height="399" width="624" src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfqUmar5ARCm7c4yBJfbV8e2jbCLD4PCI8CEYYPoJupOf0GxqvOitKgSad6_rgWkMbSk7e_Q-m8qU1fM1r_UCF8ze8pbTbTifiq91T9CCdtPdDSaztW7iWBPrc8HpYB_Vtfyzo6IA?key=wFDXEcsw7QqwrKfLfsp4Zxsi" />

Click the red ‘Delete’ button and the page will be removed from your website.

<img height="359" width="624" src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXe8dmrRdSis3p6fz-renI4NtsJK2zGN78ifG5DUOKrdTGEufJ6Byn3ButZWlMOzHGVZ1ZFETbYVApxef4D-gjnHtqNf9sjEETn7VBsRKasOIED0vqYXCGQLRMZZI7l5hksJFykh6Q?key=wFDXEcsw7QqwrKfLfsp4Zxsi" />

Congratulations, you just de-cluttered your website!
